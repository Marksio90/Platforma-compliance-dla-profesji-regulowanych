## 1. LEXISNEXIS

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **URL** | https://www.lexisnexis.com |
| **Primary Offering** | Legal research platform (Lexis+, Lexis Advance) |
| **Content Publishing Tools** | **Martindale-Hubbell Legal Articles** - allows lawyers to publish articles for thought leadership; articles distributed to 40,000+ registered users; 14,000+ searchable articles database. No self-service content publishing platform for general lawyer marketing. |

| **Target Market** | All firm sizes; 150,000+ legal professionals; primarily research-focused |
| **Regulatory Compliance for Ads** | No automated state bar advertising compliance |

## 3. LAWLYTICS

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **URL** | https://www.lawlytics.com |
| **Primary Offering** | Website platform built exclusively for law firms |
| **Content Publishing** | Built-in content generator for practice area pages; drag-and-drop editor; integrated blogging platform; unlimited pages; video hosting; attorney-friendly WYSIWYG editor. Practice area authoring tool covers 10+ areas (bankruptcy, business law, criminal defense, employment, estate planning, family law, immigration, personal injury, real estate, SSD). |
| **Compliance Features** | **Automatic bar compliance features** - disclaimers auto-generated based on state bar requirements; attorney bio pages follow ethical guidelines; case results templates follow ethical guidelines. **This is their key differentiator.** |
| **Pricing** | ~$179-225/mo depending on firm size/location (annual vs monthly). Example: 2-5 lawyer firm in Arizona = $179/mo annual or $225/mo monthly. No long-term contracts. |
| **Target Market** | Solo practitioners and small to mid-sized US law firms |
| **Regulatory Compliance for Ads** | **YES** - Automatic state bar compliance disclaimers |
