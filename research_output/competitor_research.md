## 1. DOXIMITY

**URL:** https://www.doximity.com

**Description:** "LinkedIn for doctors" - Largest digital network of medical professionals in the US. Combines verified professional networking with workflow tools (telehealth, fax, messaging, scheduling) and monetizes through pharmaceutical marketing and hospital recruiting.

**Pricing:**
- **For physicians:** Free to join and use core features
- **Dialer Pro (telehealth):** $24/user/month for individual clinicians
- **Dialer Enterprise:** Custom pricing for hospitals/health systems (includes BAA, EMR integration, SSO, unlimited group visits)
- **Marketing Solutions (for pharma):** Custom enterprise pricing. FY2024 revenue ~$475M. 120 customers with $500K+ revenue each.
- **Hiring Solutions:** Subscription-based for hospitals/health systems

**HIPAA/Medical Regulatory Compliance:**
- ✅ HIPAA-compliant messaging and telehealth tools
- ✅ Validated through external auditing procedures
- ✅ Institutional BAA available for Enterprise plans
- ✅ Doximity GPT is HIPAA-compliant generative AI

**Content Publishing Capabilities for Doctors:**
- Medical newsfeed with curated content
- Doximity GPT (AI writing assistant) for drafting patient letters, insurance appeals, patient education materials
- PeerCheck™ - physician-verified resources
- No direct "publish to public" platform for doctors; content is curated by Doximity

**Target Market:**
- Primary: US physicians (85% of US physicians registered), NPs, PAs, pharmacists
- Paying customers: Pharmaceutical manufacturers, health systems, medical recruiters
- Geographic focus: Primarily United States

**Key Sources:**
- Doximity 10K SEC filing (2024)
- Doximity investor deck (Q1 FY2026)
- https://blog.doximity.com/articles/Free-Medical-AI-Tools-Are-They-HIPAA-Compliant-And-Safe-for-Doctors-To-Use
- https://umbrex.com/resources/company-profiles/doximity/

## 2. SERMO

**URL:** https://www.sermo.com

**Description:** World's leading physician-only social network. Blends peer-to-peer social collaboration with medical education, market research, and pharmaceutical engagement. 1M+ verified physicians, APPs, and pharmacists across 150 countries.

**Pricing:**
- **For physicians:** Free to join
- **For pharma/marketers:** Custom enterprise pricing (not publicly disclosed)
- **Sermo Pages (pharma brand pages):** Free to set up; media placement options available (network posts, promotional ads, newsletter placements)
- **Market research surveys:** Physicians paid honoraria for participation (collectively earned $20M+ in 2024)

**HIPAA/Medical Regulatory Compliance:**
- ✅ Triple-verified, credentialed community
- ✅ Built for pharma compliance: 20+ years experience
- ✅ Built-in Important Safety Information (ISI) and references
- ✅ Clear separation of sponsored vs. organic content
- ✅ Geography-based access controls for content
- ✅ Draft/preview/publish/unpublish controls for pharma pages

**Content Publishing Capabilities for Doctors:**
- Peer-to-peer posting, commenting, case collaboration
- Polls and voting on medical topics
- Sermo Rounds educational events
- Knowledge Repositories for "always-on" learning
- CME (Continuing Medical Education) access
- Real-world case discussions

**Target Market:**
- Primary: Physicians, Advanced Practice Providers, pharmacists
- Paying customers: Pharmaceutical companies, medical device companies, market research firms
- Geographic reach: 150 countries

**Key Sources:**
- https://www.sermo.com/resources/physician-compensation-report-2024-2025/
- https://www.franklypharma.com/blog/sermo

## 3. FIGURE 1

**URL:** https://figure1.com (app-based, Toronto-based)

**Description:** Medical case-sharing platform ("Instagram for doctors") - photo and knowledge-sharing social networking app for medical professionals to discuss cases and collaborate.

**Pricing:**
- **Basic app:** Free for healthcare professionals
- **Premium/Figure 1 Plus:** Not clearly priced in public sources; may have institutional licensing

**HIPAA/Medical Regulatory Compliance:**
- ⚠️ Does NOT store patient-identifying information
- Cases are de-identified and do not contain names, addresses, or other PHI
- Platform states cases "do not fall under privacy regulations around the world such as HIPAA"
- Community reporting system for potential privacy breaches
- Reported cases are immediately pulled and reviewed
- Infringing cases are securely destroyed

**Content Publishing Capabilities for Doctors:**
- Share medical cases with images (de-identified)
- Comment and discuss cases with peers
- Browse cases by specialty
- Medical knowledge sharing
- NOT a general content publishing platform - focused on case sharing only

**Target Market:**
- Healthcare professionals globally
- Medical students, residents, practicing physicians
- All specialties

**Key Sources:**
- https://blog.hushmail.com/blog/figure-1-a-photo-and-knowledge-sharing-app-for-doctors
- https://patientnotes.ai/resources/medical-apps-healthcare-providers

**Content Publishing Capabilities for Doctors:**
- **Perspectives section:** Doctors can submit opinion pieces (max 800 words)

- **Freelance features:** Timely stories about practice of medicine (800-3,000 words)
- **News articles:** Journal article coverage, conference reporting
- 10,000 KOL contributors publish 60,000+ articles annually
- 30+ physician discussion boards

**Target Market:**
- Healthcare professionals globally
- 13M+ active members across 30+ specialties
- Pharmaceutical industry partners

**Key Sources:**

## KEY GAPS & OPPORTUNITIES

1. **No true "LinkedIn + Medium for doctors" exists** - Doximity is closest but lacks robust doctor publishing tools
2. **Content compliance for doctor-generated content** is underserved - most platforms are either pharma tools (Veeva) or reference apps (Epocrates, UpToDate)
3. **HIPAA-compliant social publishing** is nascent - SocialClimb and eClincher are emerging but limited
4. **Professional medical content monetization** for individual doctors is not well-addressed
5. **European/regulated professions** content compliance platform appears to be a blue ocean opportunity
